Bader College opens its doors to short-term programming and continuing education jlw2 Tue, 07/07/2026 - 00:14 Students in the courtyard at Bader College. Lifelong learning initiatives The academic programs are taking place alongside a growing number of continuing education initiatives at the castle that welcome professionals and lifelong learners to campus. These include heritage craft workshops, such as landscape painting classes inspired by Winston Churchill’s paintings of the castle grounds and a needlework session featuring a specially created Bayeux Tapestry-inspired design.
